Example of reflection and refraction

Reflection and refraction of light occur all around us. Mirrors reflect light, while objects like glass refract light.





Reflection of light rays

Different surfaces reflect light in different ways. Reflection from smooth surfaces is called specular reflection. Reflection from rough surfaces is called diffuse reflection





Prism

White light, when passed through a prism, is dispersed into red, orange, yellow, blue, indigo and violet





Refraction of Light (Part 1)

Refraction of light can only take place in transparent media suck air, water, vacuum (in space, glass, diamond and others.

Light travels at different speed in different media. Less dense medium: Faster speed Denser medium: slower speed.
